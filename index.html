Who we are

We're a small team of computer science majors interested in helping others by offering fun, accessible experiences that encourage people like you to live active, healthy lifestyles. This website is our latest project, brought to you through the MHacks hackathon at the University of Michigan in Ann Arbor. We hope you enjoy Push-Up Ninja, and look forward to growing with you.


How to play

This website requires the Myo armband, a wearable gadget that reads electrical impulses from the muscles of the forearm and transmits them via bluetooth to our software. All you need to do is:

  1. Fit the armband onto the thickest part of your right arm (even if you're left handed) with the micro-usb port facing away from you. Make sure the glowing blue Myo logo is facing up and slightly to the right.
  2. Let your arm hang by your side, then raise your forearm and wave your hand outward and to the right. If the band vibrates and the blue light starts flashing quickly, you've just successfully synced the device.
  3. Navigate down to the Start button at the bottom of this page. Make sure you're ready, and then assume a straight-armed planking position. When the indicator on the screen tells you to start, begin your Push-Ups. Remember to keep a steady pace and not to over-exert yourself -- your health is important to us!
  4. Come back whenever you want to beat your high score! It'll be right here waiting for you.
